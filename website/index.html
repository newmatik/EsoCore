Edge-native intelligence and observability

EdgeSentinel turns raw edge data into actionable insight.

EdgeSentinel is an open‑source platform that makes machines easy to monitor and understand. Plug‑and‑play sensors capture what matters, on‑device AI spots issues early, and everything keeps working—even when the network is down. It connects cleanly to your existing systems and includes fleet setup, updates, alerts, and dashboards—so you prevent downtime and keep full ownership of your data.
